Your values, on your side.

Every app profiles you for someone else — the feed, the advertiser, the algorithm. Mūla is an AI that learns what you actually value, writes it into cards you keep, and works for no one but you. It runs inside your own AI agent, on your own machine. There is no server.

The sovereignty ladder

Our rule: decentralization arrives exactly where trust exits — and not a step before.

  • Now — you are here, and it's further up than most products ever get. Cards on your device, in plain files you own. Nothing to export because nothing ever left.
  • Next: your keys. Cards signed on your device — unforgeable, portable, provable anywhere without showing the rest.
  • Then: private discovery. Two wallets find common ground without either revealing its contents — math instead of a middleman.
  • Eventually: no operator anywhere. A public anchor for identity and revocation only. No content on any chain, ever.
